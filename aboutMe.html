Future goals and dreams

On a personal level, I feel strongly that I should help others less fortunate than me, to learn how to code. This is the power of technology, that through learning about computers and code, I can affect positive change in the lives of those around me. I am willing to share what I know with others, and even if I can simply make a positive impact on the life of one other person I would be happy.

On a professional level, I aim to create websites of the highest quality. I always strive to meet high standards: easy-to-use interfaces between user and product; meeting all design specifications; cross-browser compatibility; and so much more. To reach this goal, I have to keep learning to code, getting better and better with every iteration. My dream is to become better than I am right now and know more than I currently do. I know that the only way to get there is through being challenged, I might not always know where to begin when I start, but I learn along the way - and that’s what keeps me going, keeps me striving for more.

I love being a front-end developer and I want to be an even better one. A huge part of my goal is related to design and unleashing my creativity - I think that UX and UI are fields that are very important and I would love to learn more than code - I want to take projects from the ground up, making websites that are beautiful as well as functional.
