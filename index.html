About me


My name is Livhuwani Mukheli, I am an ingenious and determined Front-End Developer and eager to offer superb service. I am able to work well both on my own initiative or as part of a team.

I have hunger for knowledge and determination to turn information into action. I am a graduate of the African Development Academy. I always have a good time while doing what I love and I love what I do.

I believe mindfulness in the workplace is key to success.




I am an ADA Graduate!

ADA - the African Development Academy - is a new program created to teach young women of colour how to code, bringing change to the tech workforce. My journey with ADA began in June 2019, when I was called for a bootcamp. Truthfully I felt as though I had no chance whatsoever. All I knew was that I had fun with the games we played and challenges we took at the bootcamp. I was one of the top 12 women who was selected to take a 11 month learnership.

The focus of the learnership was to help us become Front-End Developers. It was all amazing and wonderful at the same time. I got to learn how to create websites and I overcame so many challenges. It was a great opportunity to learn something new and I learnt a lot during my time at ADA. There is so much more to tell when it comes to my coding journey, click below to read more.
